Minimum and Maximum Installation Costs

The cost of installation depends on the scale and type of system:

  • Residential Solar Panel System: ₹40,000 - ₹1,50,000
  • Commercial Solar Panel System: ₹5,00,000 - ₹50,00,000
  • Small Wind Turbine: ₹2,00,000 - ₹10,00,000
  • Large Wind Turbine: ₹50,00,000 and above

Space Required

Approximate space requirements for efficient installation:

  • 1 kW Solar Panel System: ~100 sq. ft.
  • Small Residential Wind Turbine: ~500 sq. ft. (clear area)
  • Large Commercial Wind Turbine: ~1 acre

Electricity Generation Potential

Electricity generation depends on factors such as sunlight availability and wind speed. Approximate generation potential:

  • 1 kW Solar Panel System: 4-5 units per day
  • Small Wind Turbine: 10-15 units per day
  • Large Wind Turbine: 100-150 units per day
